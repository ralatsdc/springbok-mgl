     SECTION 1. Section 3 of chapter 343 of the acts of 1935 is hereby amended by striking out the words “board of selectmen” and inserting in place thereof the following words:- select board.
     SECTION 2. Said chapter 343 is hereby further amended by striking out section 4 and inserting in place thereof the following section:-
                Section 4. Any person injured in their property by any action of said select board under this act may recover damages from said town under said chapter 79.
     SECTION 3. Said chapter 343 is hereby further amended by striking out section 5 and inserting in place thereof the following section:-
                Section 5. The town shall, by vote, determine what proportion of the cost of said system or systems of sewerage and sewage disposal the town shall pay, provided that it shall pay not more than 25 per cent. In providing for the payment of the remaining portion of the cost of said system or systems or for the use of said system or systems, the town, through its select board acting as the sewer commissioners, may avail itself of any or all of the methods permitted by General Laws and the provisions of said General Laws relative to the assessment, apportionment, division, reassessment, abatement and collection of sewer assessments, to liens therefor and to interest thereon, shall apply to assessments made under this act. The collector of taxes of said town shall certify the payment or payments of such assessments or apportionments thereof to the said select board, which shall preserve a record thereof.
     SECTION 4. Section 8 of said chapter 343 is hereby amended by striking out the words “board of selectmen” and inserting in place thereof the following words:- select board.
     SECTION 5. Section 9 of said chapter 343 is hereby amended by striking out the words “board of selectmen” and inserting in place thereof the following words:- select board.
     SECTION 6. The first sentence of section 10 of said chapter 343 is hereby amended by striking out the words “not exceeding twenty dollars,” and inserting in place thereof the following words:- in accordance with the General Laws and any bylaw adopted by the town,.
     SECTION 7. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
